The ProoVer 2026 competition will take place on **27 July 2026**, as part of the FLoC Olympics held during [FLoC 2026](https://www.floc26.org/).  
The infrastructure will be shared with [CASC](https://tptp.org/CASC/), and both competitions will run in parallel.  
The competition setup and execution environment will follow the same principles as [CASC](https://tptp.org/CASC/).  
We thank [Geoff Sutcliffe](https://www.cs.miami.edu/home/geoff/) for his support and for providing the competition infrastructure.


## Deadlines and Important Dates (AoE)
  * End of June 2026: [System registration](../registration.md)
  * Beginning of July 2026: [System submission](../registration.md)
  * End of July 2026: ProoVer Competition at [IJCAR2026](https://www.floc26.org/ijcar), part of [FLOC2026](https://www.floc26.org/)



## Results

Results will be announced during [FLoC 2026](https://www.floc26.org/) and published on this website shortly after the competition.  
The ranking will follow the official grading scheme described in the [competition rules](../competition-setup.md).
